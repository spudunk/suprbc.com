About

SuperB Construction is a second generation, family owned company passed down from father to son. We are currently serving Kelso, Longview, Chehalis, Centralia, and nearby areas.

Our specialties are decks, fences, garages and pole buildings. We're happy to provide estimates on other custom building projects. Give us a call or email for your free estimate!

Decks

Fencing

As some of the first projects our team ever did together, we really enjoy fencing projects. It's a great opportunity for us to really showcase our skills and make a drastic visible difference on your property.

Garages

Whether it's an interior remodel or a complete new building, we're ready to take on the job and bring both quality finish work and functionality to your working space.
